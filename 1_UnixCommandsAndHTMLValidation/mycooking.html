Chocolate Mousse

al dente
A correct degree of doneness for pasta and vegetables.
baste
Brush oil around item to be cooked to prevent dryness.
pinenut
Also called pignoli.
The Ingredients
  • 100 g. flour
  • 10 g. sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • salt, pepper
The procedure
  1. Mix dry ingredients thoroughly.
  2. Pour in wet ingredients.
  3. Mix for 10 minautes.
  4. Bake for one hour at 300 degrees.
Notes:
The recipe may be improved by adding raisins
